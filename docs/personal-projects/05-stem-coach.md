# STEM Coach

October 2025

I assisted in mentoring students from Koforidua Secondary Technical on a smart dispenser project that used voice activation and hand-motion gestures to dispense water.

## Project Overview

- Guided students through the design and implementation of an assistive dispenser system.
- Helped them integrate sensors and control logic for gesture-based operation.
- Provided coaching on prototype development, testing, and presentation.

## Outcome

- The project was showcased at the Innovate Koforidua Challenge organized by Eastern Tech Hub.
- The experience strengthened my mentoring skills and my ability to communicate technical ideas clearly.
- It reaffirmed my interest in robotics applications that help people and support accessibility.
