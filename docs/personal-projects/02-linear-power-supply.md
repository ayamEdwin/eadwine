# 5V Linear Power Supply

This project grew from my Analogue Electronics I coursework and later became a practical personal design exercise. I designed and simulated a 5V linear power supply using Multisim, then recreated the design in KiCAD to improve my PCB layout and routing skills.

## Project Highlights

- Designed a stable 5V linear regulator circuit suitable for powering sensitive electronics.
- Simulated the circuit behavior in Multisim to verify voltage regulation, thermal response, and load performance.
- Re-created the full schematic and PCB layout in KiCAD, applying best practices for power routing, component placement, and grounding.
- Prepared the design for future fabrication to support my upcoming robotic hand manipulator project.

## Learning Outcomes

- Strengthened knowledge of analog electronics, voltage regulation, and power supply design.
- Practiced PCB schematic capture and layout techniques in KiCAD.
- Understood the importance of thermal management and noise reduction in power-system design.
- Built confidence in designing supporting electronics for robotics and embedded systems projects.
