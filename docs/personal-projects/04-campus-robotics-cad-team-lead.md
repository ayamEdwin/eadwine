# Campus Robotics CAD Team Lead

In my second year, I served as the CAD team lead for the Campus Robotics Club. I supervised the mechanical design of a smart bin system showcased at an engineering fair held at Accra Technical University.

## Role and Responsibilities

- Led the mechanical design and CAD modeling effort for the smart bin project.
- Designed and 3D-printed a battery casing and an ultrasonic sensor holder.
- Coordinated with electrical and software teammates to ensure the mechanical design supported the full system.
- Helped prepare the project for demonstration at the robotics exhibition.

## Impact

- The project showcased practical engineering teamwork and cross-disciplinary integration.
- It improved my CAD and additive manufacturing skills.
- It strengthened my ability to design functional enclosures and supports for robotics systems.
